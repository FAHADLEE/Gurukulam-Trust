Vietnam

Vietnam is the country occupying the eastern portion of mainland Southeast Asia. Its capital, Hanoi, is located in the north, while the country's largest city, Ho Chi Minh City (formerly Saigon), is in the south. The country is home to a scintillating Geography, an eventful history and many diverse cultures. Vietnamese people are known for their rich hospitality throughout the world. COURSE

The six-year program is structured around THREE themes

CLINICIAN

We start with basic sciences ( Biophysics, Chemistry and Biology) in year one and move to pre-clinical sciences followed by clinical sciences.

PROFESSIONAL

This theme will introduce professional attributes as a medical student through the medical ethics and psychology unit in year one. As you progress into year two, this theme will be embedded into all the units offered in the MD program with clear expectations required of a medical student in the academic, clinical and social settings.

RESEARCHER

Bioinformatics in year one sets the foundation of this theme with some content in the unit “ Psychology and Medical Ethics”. From year two, onwards there are units offered in the Foundations of Research stream, Evidence-based Practice in clinical units leading to a research project that is required for each student to graduate.

Duy Tan University

Duy Tan University is located in the heart of sunny Danang, on the Pacific Ocean. The University has steadily developed, sharing its ambition with the city to create a bustling, modern, international metropolis. Founded by the decree of the Prime Minister of Vietnam on the November 11th, 1994, Duy Tan University is the first and the largest private, multidisciplinary and diversified institution in Central Vietnam.
