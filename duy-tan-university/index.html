About University

Duy Tan University is located in the heart of sunny Danang, on the Pacific Ocean. The University has steadily developed, sharing its ambition with the city to create a bustling, modern, international metropolis. Founded by the decree of the Prime Minister of Vietnam on the November 11th, 1994, Duy Tan University is the first and the largest private, multidisciplinary and diversified institution in Central Vietnam.

PRE-CLINICAL SIMULATION PRACTICE CENTER

(MEDICAL CENTER SIMULATION)

Duy Tan University is trying to create a friendly learning environment that best supports students focusing on training about practical skills, aiming at improving health care for the patients.

It has been designed as a simulated hospital environment with state-of-the-art facilities and a wide range of modern simulators and equipment which are always updated to meet the standards of simulation-based training around the world.
