About Us

HongBang
International University

Since its establishment with a single faculty in 1997, HongBang International University (HIU) has grown manifold to establish 10 faculties now. At HongBang, parents and students have a wide variety of modernistic courses, including medicine, pharmacy, Odontostomatology, Nursing, Physiotherapy, Rehabilitation Engineering, etc. The university boasts a team of highly experienced academic staff which is complemented by an exquisite infrastructure. The university environment is designed to accommodate students from diverse backgrounds and various countries from across the world. Students are given complete freedom to express themselves and assured to be given individual attention at every stage.

Signing Ceremony

Aieraa Overseas Studies became the first Indian organisation to tie up with a Vietnam-based educational institution. The HongBang International University and Aieraa collaborate to give the best medical education to Indian medical aspirants at an affordable fee structure. Thus, HIU Vietnam has opened its door to admit Indian students to their Faculty of Medicine programme.

The following dignitaries were present during the signing ceremony

  • Ms. Deepa R. – Director of Aieraa Overseas Studies
  • Dr. Do Manh Cuong – Chairman of the Council of HongBang International University
  • Prof. Dr. Pham Van Linh – Rector, Assoc. Prof. Dr. Le Khac Cuong
  • Ms. Tran Thuy Tram Quyen – Vice-Rector of the school

In particular, the ceremony was witnessed by the Consulate General of India in Ho Chi Minh City – Mr. Madan Mohan Sethi, Ambassador Extraordinary Plenipotentiary of Vietnam in India – Mr. Pham Sanh Chau, Assoc. Dr. Thai Ba Can – Deputy General Director of Nguyen Hoang Group.
